5C Laundry

Mobile UI/UX

5C Laundry is a laundry service for students at the Claremont Colleges. For a couple years I was the lead iOS designer and developer for the app. I decided to revisit the design without having to consider the limitations of also developing the app.

Initial Design

The initial designs were done with the lens of also having to develop the app as well.

Screenshots from the app store

The Redesign

I decided to redesign the app to more clearly organize information and lend the app more credibility through a cleaner and more appropriate design. My main goals in this redesign were to redesign the "schedule" page, give the app a new menu layout, and create a new process process for choosing a plan.

Sketches of the redesigned schedule and map pages

Final Designs

The final designs have a more modern feel and will be more clearly navigable by the target audience (college age students).
